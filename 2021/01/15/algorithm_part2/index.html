lecture 7 Introduction to DFS

  • 简介：在图的基础上从细节上介绍了dfs，在dfs的基础上讲解了拓扑排序，一般讨论图默认有向图，重点讨论了下无向图中的双向连通分量
  • 一些概念方便理解dfs
    • color
      • white：还没被发现的点
      • gray：已经被发现的点，但是它们子树的搜索还在继续
      • black：所有的子树包括他自己已被探索完毕
    • event
      • disc(u):变gray时间；finish(u):变black时间
    • int(u)
      • the interval [disc(u), f inish(u)]
    • 从图的角度，dfs形成了一个森林，森林中有这样4种边
      • 对于无向图，这里定义它没有forward edge & cross edge
    • 一些性质
      • (v desc u) <=>(v被发现是u is gray)
      • 当我们探索(u,v)时，u is gray,v为不同颜色对应不同种类的(u,v)
      • 关于int(u)的一些性质
      • 关于dfs与circle：Graph, G, has a cycle <=> every DFS has a back edge
    • 算法
      • O(m+n)
  • Topologiccal sorting
    • definition
    • 结论
  • undirected graph
    • connected/connected component(default maximal)
    • Biconnected Components
      • 定义
        • An articulation point of a connected graph G is a vertex v such that G − v is disconnected
        • A biconnected graph is a connected graph that has no articulation points
        • A biconnected component (BCC) (or block) of a connected undirected graph is a maximal biconnected subgraph.
      • 性质
        • any ugraph other than K2 is biconnected iff(if and only if) for any two distinct nodes u and v there are two vertex-disjoint paths from u to v
        • Biconnected components are edge-disjoint
          • 推论：两个双向连通分量的唯一一个交点即为articulation point
        • 对于DFS tree T，判断point是否为articulation point，r为根节点，u为非根节点
          • r is an articulation point iff r has at least two children
          • u is an articulation point iff for some child v of u, low(v) ≥ dsc(u)
